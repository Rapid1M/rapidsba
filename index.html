FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can funding happen?

+
Depending on the lender and qualification, some options may be available in 24–72 hours after review.

Do I need perfect credit?

+
Not necessarily. Funding options vary based on multiple business factors including revenue, time in business, and financial profile.

What types of funding are available?

+
Working capital, equipment financing, revenue-based financing, and other alternative lending options.

Is there any obligation?

+
No. Submitting a request simply allows us to review potential options for your business.

What businesses qualify?

+
Most industries are eligible, including service-based businesses, retail, construction, transportation, and more.

How do I receive funding?

+
If matched with an option, you'll be guided through the lender's application and approval process.
